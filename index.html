Upcycling Luxury

Breathing New Spirit Into Discarded Bones

At Wasana, we believe true luxury should not cost the earth. Every year, massive amounts of animal bone waste from food processing are discarded, posing environmental challenges. We actively reclaim this durable, natural material, sterilizing and carving it into beautiful treasures.

By pairing ancient Balinese spiritual symbolism with a refined modern aesthetic, we support local craftsmen and offer high-end accessories that hold deep artistic, cultural, and ecological value.

🌱

Support Local Communities

Every purchase directly sustains local Balinese carvers and their families, preserving generation-old traditional techniques.

The Ritual

The Alchemy of Wasana

How raw waste is elevated into pristine high-end symbols of nature and cultural heritage.

🏺
Phase I

Reclamation

We source raw, ethically-discarded animal bones directly from agricultural waste streams, diverting them from landfills.

✨
Phase II

Purification

The bone is thoroughly boiled, degreased, and safely sterilized using organic agents to achieve a pristine bone-white ivory texture.

✍️
Phase III

The Carving

Master Balinese craftsmen painstakingly sketch and chisel detailed animal and natural shapes into the raw surface by hand.

📿
Phase IV

Assembly

The polished amulets are threaded with premium organic cotton cords, robust wood, or volcanic stones, completing the adornment.
